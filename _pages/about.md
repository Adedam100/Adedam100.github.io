Hi, I'm Adedamola Adetola, a First-Class Mechanical Engineering graduate from Abiola Ajimobi Technical University, distinguished by strong academic excellence and practical engineering competence. Throughout my academic journey, I combined theoretical knowledge with hands-on application, actively organizing tutorials and supporting departmental coordination as Assistant Departmental Representative. 

Professionally, I have extensive experience in manufacturing, maintenance, machining, and aerospace applications. Currently, I'm serving as a UAV Technician at AeroTech Services Limited, where I apply my mechanical expertise to drone assembly, diagnostics, structural repairs, and flight operations support. Certified by international aviation bodies
Including the European Union Aviation Safety Agency and the Federal Aviation Administration, I combine mechanical engineering proficiency with advanced UAV systems knowledge. 

I am open to collaborations in manufacturing or machining fields and Aerial Robotics research, particularly in ways to improve existing technology and form new ones for the benefit of Man. Let’s work together to drive impactful advancements in this exciting field!
